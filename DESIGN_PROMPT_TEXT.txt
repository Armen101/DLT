DUBAI DRIVER LICENSE THEORY APP - UI/UX DESIGN BRIEF

==================================================
PROJECT OVERVIEW
==================================================

Create a mobile app for Dubai driving license theory exam preparation. Modern, clean design with multi-language support (English, Arabic RTL, Hindi/Urdu). Target audience: aspiring drivers in Dubai, ages 18-50, multicultural background.

==================================================
KEY FEATURES & MONETIZATION
==================================================

FREE VERSION:
- 1 free topic (Traffic Signs) with full access
- All app features available (practice, pin, review mistakes)
- 14 other topics locked with clear lock icons

RENTAL MODEL (Google Play In-App Purchase):
- 30 Days: $9.99 (Most Popular badge)
- 90 Days: $24.99 (Best Value badge)  
- 1 Year: $49.99 (Save 50% badge)
- Unlocks all 15 topics (1500+ questions)
- Rental renews automatically

TECHNICAL NOTES
==================================================

GOOGLE PLAY IN-APP PURCHASE:
- Billing Library v5+
- Product IDs: rental_30days, rental_90days, rental_1year
- Product Type: Subscription with rental period
- Receipt validation required
- Store expiry date locally and server-side

BACKEND INTEGRATION:
- Check subscription status on app launch
- Endpoints: /api/subscription/verify, /status, /activate, /expiry
- Lock/unlock topics based on active rental
- Free topic (Traffic Signs) always accessible

DATA STORAGE:
- Pinned questions: Client-side (AsyncStorage/SharedPreferences)
- Subscription status: Server-side with local cache
- Rental expiry: Server-verified, locally displayed
